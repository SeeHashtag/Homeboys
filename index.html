About

Our Founder

Jacob Cutino has been in the restaurant industry for over 20 years. During that time, he worked his way up from washing dishes to eventually becoming a chef. After years of experience cooking, Jacob decided to channel his passion for food into starting a company that is transforming the Phoenix culinary scene: Homeboy's Hot Sauce. These days you'll find Jacob in the kitchen experimenting with different flavors trying to develop the best hot sauce you'll ever taste!

JACOB

History

Homeboy's Hot Sauces was founded in March 2015. Our sauces are Jacob's version of a sauce his Trinidadian stepfather used to make. The original hot sauce recipe had no quantities just of list of ingredients that combined to create the most flavorful hot sauce he had ever tried. It took Jacob over two years to develop and fine tune those recipes into the sauces we produce now. We started selling our hot sauce at Uptown Farmers Market. Since that time we have expanded into numerous locations around Arizona and across the United States.
